ABOUT US

Hi, my name is Mona. I am a mother, and a wife. In 2017, I made the decision to opt for a 100% plant-based lifestyle. I made the decision to change because of the tremendous health and lifestyle benefits. I am also raising my children, from birth, on a 100% plant-based diet. As a result, I have experienced the power of healing through plants not only as a food source but as an alternative to modern medicine. 

With the creation of Back to Basics, we try to share and encourage others to make good decisions about their wellbeing by teaching and sharing our experiences. 

Mona Lisa Sop-Lepen
